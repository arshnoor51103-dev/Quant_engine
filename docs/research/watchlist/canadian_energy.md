## Why This Sector Exists in the Watchlist

The current Tier 1 universe has **zero direct energy exposure.** The TSX Composite (XIC.TO) has indirect exposure through its sector weights (~18–20% energy historically), but the growth bucket has no dedicated energy position.

Canada is a major oil and gas producer. Energy is the second-largest sector on the TSX after financials. This is a significant portfolio blind spot for a Canadian-focused strategy.

**Potential role:** Diversification within the growth bucket — energy tends to be negatively correlated with tech during risk-off/inflation events, which is the exact regime where HXQ.TO and CHPS.TO would decline.
